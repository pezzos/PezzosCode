# AGENTS.md

Purpose of this folder:
- Keep chronological logs of what happened during and after development.

Key files:
- `docs/03-logs/implementation-log.md`: what changed and why.
- `docs/03-logs/decision-log.md`: key decisions and rationale.
- `docs/03-logs/bug-log.md`: bugs, fixes, and prevention.
- `docs/03-logs/validation-log.md`: post-launch outcomes.
- `docs/03-logs/insights.md`: distilled learnings and patterns.
